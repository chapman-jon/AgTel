# 2025 Agronomy Trial Program — Season Summary (internal)

*Agronomy Research Group — L. Amaechi, program coordinator*
*Distribution: research group, consulting leadership, product marketing*

## Program shape

Trial plots in 10 US states and 3 Canadian provinces this season (a mix of new
stakes and multi-year sites carried forward in BST from prior seasons), plus the
long-running Kalgoorlie dryland series in Western Australia. As usual the North
American trial plots are managed in BST by the research group; consultants hosting
a trial on cooperator ground should coordinate with us before entering anything.
(The Kalgoorlie series predates the acquisition and is still tracked in the local
research team's own workbooks rather than as BST plot records — consolidating it
has been on the roadmap for three years and counting.)

## Headline results (full statistical write-ups on the research share)

- **Torrent Duo vs Torrent SC (white mold, 28 soybean sites):** Duo averaged +2.1
  bu/ac over SC at the same timing window. Response concentrated in the 14 sites
  with measurable disease pressure; flat elsewhere. Recommendation unchanged:
  scout first, don't blanket-apply.
- **Vantiva XC rate ladder (waterhemp, 9 sites):** the 0.75x rate broke at 4 of 9
  sites. We will not support below-label rates in consultant plans, full stop.
- **AgTel N-32 split application (corn, 17 sites):** split shows +3.4 bu/ac vs
  single pre-plant at sites with sand fractions >40%, negligible elsewhere.
  Placement guidance going into the winter training deck.
- **RootSet Starter 6-24-6 (12 cold-soil sites):** visual response in 10 of 12,
  yield response in 3. Marketing has been asked (again) not to quote the visual
  numbers without the yield caveat.
- **Kalgoorlie dryland series, year 7:** moisture-limited year; treatment
  separation minimal. Series continues per the acquisition-era commitment to the
  Western Australia grower group.

## Operational notes

- Trial plot subdivision continues to confuse new consultants: a trial staked out
  of a production field is its own plot record for the season. If you see a
  "field" that looks like a sliver of another field, check the use code before
  asking us if it's a duplicate.
- Two cooperator disputes this season (herbicide carryover claims). Both resolved;
  reminder that trial agreements must be countersigned before first application.
- 2026 program planning starts August; site nominations to L. Amaechi by July 31.
