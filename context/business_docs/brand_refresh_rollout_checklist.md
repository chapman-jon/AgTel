*Marketing Communications — H. Delacroix*

Phase 1 (digital properties, HQ signage) is complete. Phase 2 covers field and
retail touchpoints. Area leads own the checklist for their territories.

## Checklist per location

- [ ] Exterior signage swap scheduled with the vendor (lead time ~6 weeks;
      Canadian sites add customs time for the sign panels)
- [ ] Truck door decals — replace at next scheduled service, not before
      (fleet admin coordination; do NOT peel old decals yourself, the paint
      comes with them)
- [ ] Retire old-logo apparel from customer-facing use by Aug 1 (staff may
      keep it for personal use; the research group asked and yes, trial-day
      polos count as customer-facing)
- [ ] Grower-facing templates: pull from the new template library only.
      Old PowerPoint decks with the 2011 logo keep surfacing at field days —
      area leads please do a sweep of shared drives
- [ ] Yard flags/banners: order through the marketing portal (min order 10)
