# 2026-27 Benefits Open Enrollment — Employee FAQ

*Human Resources — benefits team. Enrollment window: 2026-06-01 to 2026-06-19.*

**Q: What's changing this year?**
A: Three things. (1) The HSA-eligible plan employer contribution increases to
$900 individual / $1,800 family. (2) A new voluntary legal services plan is
offered. (3) Dental network switches carriers — most local dentists are in
both networks, but check yours.

**Q: Do I need to do anything if I want to keep my current elections?**
A: Yes, for FSA/HSA contributions only — those never roll over automatically.
Everything else re-enrolls as-is.

**Q: I'm in Canada / Australia. Does this apply to me?**
A: No. This enrollment covers US benefits only. Canadian staff are on the
provincial supplement plan (enrollment in October); Australian staff benefits
are administered locally per the acquisition agreement — see your local HR
contact.

**Q: What happened to the wellness stipend?**
A: Folded into the flexible lifestyle account ($350/yr, now covers gym, ag
show registrations for family, and — new — home internet for field staff).

**Q: Seasonal employees?**
A: Seasonal staff working 130+ hours/month become benefits-eligible in month
two of employment (ACA rules). Managers: flag long-season hires to HR early.

**Q: Who do I ask about something specific?**
A: #hr-benefits, or the enrollment site's live chat during the window.
Please don't ask your manager; they will guess, and they will guess wrong.

Elections lock at 11:59pm CT on June 19. Late changes only with a qualifying
life event.
