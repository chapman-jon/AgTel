# Fleet & Field Vehicle Policy — 2026 Update (memo)

*Corporate Services — G. Pemberton, fleet administration*
*Effective: 2026-04-01. Supersedes the 2023 policy memo.*

Summary of changes for consultants and field staff:

1. **Replacement cycle extended** from 4 years/90k miles to 5 years/110k miles
   for half-ton trucks. Exceptions for corrosion-belt provinces (SK, MB) remain
   at 4 years on request.
2. **Personal use** of fleet vehicles continues to be permitted for incidental
   use only. The mileage app now requires trip categorization at end of week
   rather than per trip (you're welcome).
3. **UTV/ATV transport:** trailering research group equipment requires the
   updated hitch inspection (annual). Book through the service portal.
4. **EV pilot:** 12 hybrid trucks join the Iowa and Illinois pools this summer.
   Charging reimbursement follows the home-office electrical rider — talk to
   fleet admin before installing anything.
5. **Fuel cards:** the WEX-to-corporate-card migration completes in Q3. Old
   cards die September 30; if your truck still has one taped in the visor,
   swap it at your area office.

Full policy on the Corporate Services intranet page. Questions: #fleet-admin
or the service portal (queue: FLEET).
