# Expense reporting — Q3 reminders (all staff)

*Finance Shared Services — automated quarterly notice, June 2026*

With field season in full swing, the usual reminders before the Q3 close:

- **Receipts required** for any single expense over $40 USD / $50 CAD / $60 AUD.
  Photos of receipts are fine; photos of your dashboard are not (we keep
  getting these).
- **Meals with growers**: use code 6215 (customer engagement), not 6210 (staff
  meals). Include attendee count. Alcohol requires area lead approval over $75.
- **Mileage**: only for personal vehicles. Fleet trucks are fueled on card;
  claiming mileage on a fleet vehicle will bounce automatically now.
- **Per diems** apply on overnight travel only. Day trips are actuals.
- **Conference travel**: pre-approval workflow moved to the service portal
  (queue: TRAVEL). Book flights 21+ days out where possible; the exception
  report goes to the CFO monthly and yes, she reads it.
- **Currency**: submit in the currency paid; the system converts. Do not
  convert manually — double conversion is our #1 correction this year.

Q3 close calendar: expense cutoff is the 3rd business day after month end.
Late submissions roll to the following period; no exceptions during audit
fieldwork (August).

Questions to #finance-help. Please do not email individual analysts.
