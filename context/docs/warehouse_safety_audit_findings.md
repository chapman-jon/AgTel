# EHS — Distribution warehouse safety audit, findings summary

*Environmental Health & Safety — audit team*
*Sites: Council Bluffs IA, Regina SK. Audit dates: 2026-05-12 to 2026-05-16.*
*Distribution: site managers, regional ops, EHS leadership.*

## Overall

Both sites remain in good standing. 14 findings total (2 major, 12 minor),
down from 21 last cycle. Major findings require corrective action plans within
30 days.

## Major findings

| # | Site | Finding | Corrective action due |
|---|---|---|---|
| M-1 | Council Bluffs | Anhydrous ammonia transfer area: eyewash station flow below spec (tested 0.32 gpm, spec 0.4). | 2026-06-15 |
| M-2 | Regina | Racking damage in aisle 7 (forklift strike, unreported). Load capacity re-certification required. | 2026-06-15 |

## Minor findings (selected)

- Expired SDS binder sheets at both sites (digital SDS system is authoritative;
  binders being retired — recommend removing them entirely rather than
  maintaining duplicates).
- Council Bluffs: two fire extinguisher monthly checks missed in March
  (documentation, not equipment).
- Regina: high-visibility vest compliance in yard at 87% observed (target 100%).
- Seasonal staffing onboarding: 3 of 41 new hires had incomplete forklift
  certification records at time of audit (all completed during audit week).

## Notes

Peak season (fall ammonia runs) pre-audit scheduled for late August at both
sites. Site managers should close M-1/M-2 well before then.

EHS thanks both site teams for cooperation. Full report in the EHS document
system (AUD-2026-019).
